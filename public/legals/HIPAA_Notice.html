HIPAA Notice

Our commitment to protecting Protected Health Information (PHI) and maintaining compliance with federal regulations.

HIPAA Compliance Commitment

CarePro VA adheres to administrative, physical, and technical safeguards required under HIPAA to ensure the protection of sensitive healthcare data.

Use of PHI

We access and use Protected Health Information strictly as necessary to perform services on behalf of our clients, and only under authorized agreements.

Business Associate Agreements

We enter into Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) where required, clearly defining responsibilities for safeguarding PHI.

Workforce Training

All personnel receive HIPAA training and follow strict confidentiality, access control, and data protection protocols.

Security Safeguards

We implement secure systems including controlled access, encrypted communication, and restricted permissions to protect sensitive data.

Minimum Necessary Rule

Access to PHI is limited to only what is necessary for task completion, in accordance with HIPAA’s minimum necessary standard.

Incident Response

Any suspected data incident is promptly investigated and handled in accordance with federal and state regulations.

No Independent Medical Role

CarePro VA provides administrative support only and does not make clinical decisions. All medical responsibility remains with the client.

CarePro VA provides reliable medical virtual admin support so your team can stay focused on what matters most — your patients.
